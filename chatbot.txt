Bollywood is the Indian Hindi-language film industry based in Mumbai.
It is one of the largest centers of film production in the world.
The term Bollywood is a portmanteau of Bombay (the former name for Mumbai) and Hollywood.
Bollywood produces a wide array of films, including musicals, romance, action, comedy, and social dramas.
Some of the biggest stars in Bollywood include Amitabh Bachchan, Shah Rukh Khan, and Aishwarya Rai.
The industry has a significant global following, with Bollywood films being popular not only in India but also in many other countries.
